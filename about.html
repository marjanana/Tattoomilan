About

“I tattoo people to help them find out more about themselves and provide a new medium for them to express themselves.” - Milan

 

I started tattooing in 2011. I focus on black and white tattoos and comic book-inspired motifs. My oeuvre includes fantasy figures, portraits, mementos, religious deigns, symbols, and fonts. Since childhood, I have been engaged in visual arts. I read comic books, dreamed up my own original illustrations, and eventually made a couple of panels that were published in the local newspaper. A great influence on my artistic style was comic book artist, Lee Bermejo, especially his unique use of light and shade, body language and expressiveness of characters. Another source of inspiration occurred during my study of textile design at the University of Leskovac, Serbia. It was a comprehensive program that combined technology, chemistry and art, which greatly contributed to my decision to start a tattoo business. For me, however, tattooing is not simply a commercial labor. I believe that every client expresses herself/himself in the process of selecting the tattoo motif, and my job is to make the final result long-lasting and aesthetically pleasing. I therefore find it important to participate in the creative process and grow with my clients.
